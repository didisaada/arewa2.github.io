About Us

We are the first ever arewa shopping website

Our website was founded by a group of passionate individuals who wanted to share the unique culture and heritage of Northern Nigeria with the world. We grew up in this region and have always been fascinated by its rich history, diverse people, and beautiful landscapes. We started this website as a way to showcase the amazing products that are created and sold in this region, and to help support the local artisans and entrepreneurs who make them. We believe that by sharing these products with the world, we can help to preserve the traditions and customs of Northern Nigeria for generations to come. Thank you for being a part of our journey!
